📝 Official Application Portal & Process

Applications for state schemes are managed through the official portal. Follow this step-by-step process to ensure a successful application submission:

  1. Register on MPTAAS Portal: Visit the official MPTAAS website and create your Profile Registration ID by verifying your Samagra ID, Aadhaar e-KYC, and Caste Certificate.
  2. Add Domicile & Income Details: Verify your digital Domicile certificate and submit your family's annual income declaration form.
  3. Apply for Scheme: Navigate to Scholarship Application, select the scheme (Post-Matric or MMVY), enter your college registration code, and upload fee receipts.

🔔 Tip: Always match your school records exactly with your Aadhaar registry to prevent verification rejections!
