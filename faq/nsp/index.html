❓ NSP (National Scholarship Portal) FAQs | Online Registration Help

OTP issues, PFMS payment status tracking, and NSP portal guidelines.

Verify that your mobile number is actively linked to Aadhaar. OTPs are sent to your Aadhaar-registered mobile. Clear your browser cache and retry during non-peak hours.

All central sector NSP payments are cleared through the Public Financial Management System (PFMS). Visit the official PFMS portal and track payments using your Aadhaar or bank account number.

NSP categorizes schemes into Pre-Matric (Class 1-10), Post-Matric (Class 11-PhD), and Merit-cum-Means (technical/professional degrees).
