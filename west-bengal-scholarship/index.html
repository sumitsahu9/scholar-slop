📝 Official Application Portal & Process

Applications for state schemes are managed through the official portal. Follow this step-by-step process to ensure a successful application submission:

  1. Profile Registration: Visit the regional portal and complete your basic student profile registration using your Aadhaar card number.
  2. Verify Academic Credentials: Enter your previous year marks, course duration, institute code, and upload the fee details document.
  3. Submit Certificate Proofs: Upload caste, income, and domicile certificates issued by the competent regional authority.

🔔 Tip: Always match your school records exactly with your Aadhaar registry to prevent verification rejections!
