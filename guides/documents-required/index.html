📥 Comprehensive Scholarship Documents Checklist

Keep these high-resolution scanned documents (PDF or JPG under 200KB preferred) ready before starting your portal registration:

  • Active Aadhaar Card: Linked to your working mobile number for e-KYC.
  • Domicile Certificate: Verifying state residency.
  • Caste Certificate: Required for SC, ST, OBC, or Minority quotas.
  • Income Certificate: Issued by a competent Tehsildar authority in the current financial year.
  • Previous mark sheets: Board certificates verifying your passing marks.
  • Fee Receipt & Admission Proof: Verifying ongoing enrollment.
  • Bank Passbook: Linked to your Aadhaar for direct benefit transfers (DBT).
