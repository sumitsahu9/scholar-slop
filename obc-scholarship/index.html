📋 Standard Eligibility & Requirements

While each scheme has specific guidelines, most OBC scholarships require the following core qualifications:

  • Category Certification: Must possess a valid caste or community certificate issued by authorized authorities.
  • Annual Family Income Limit: Variable threshold limit (generally below ₹2.5 Lakhs for SC/ST, and below ₹1.5 Lakhs for OBC).
  • Enrollment: Must be studying full-time in recognized school, college, or polytechnic courses.

📝 Step-by-Step Portal Application Guide

To submit your application successfully, follow these standard steps on either the National Scholarship Portal (NSP) or your regional state directory portal:

  1. Profile Onboarding: Verify your Aadhaar details and generate your application login credentials.
  2. Select Scheme: Search for active schemes matching your category and enter your education course details.
  3. Upload Documents: Securely upload scanned copies of your category certificate, income declaration, previous marksheet, and fee receipt.
  4. Final Submission: Verify the correctness of your application and submit it before the listed deadline.
