🎓 Official State Scholarship Portals Overview

Most Indian states manage their own welfare disbursement programs through dedicated online portals. Understanding which portal maps to your residency is the first step in unlocking financial assistance.

Major State Portals

  • Madhya Pradesh (MP): Managed via the MPTAAS portal and the MP Scholarship Portal 2.0. Programs include MMVY, Jan Kalyan, and Gaon Ki Beti.
  • Maharashtra: Managed via the MahaDBT (Mahasharera) portal, offering comprehensive post-matric stipends for SC/ST/OBC students.
  • Uttar Pradesh (UP): Managed via the UP Scholarship Portal (Saksham), handling massive pre-matric and post-matric disbursements.
  • West Bengal: Managed through Oasis, SVMCM, and Kanyashree portals for minority and merit awards.
