UGC PG Merit Scholarship for University Rank Holders

⏰ Open

Central • General / OBC / SC / ST

Total Benefit Amount ₹3,100 per month for 2 years

Important Note: Ensure all documents are scanned clearly and your profile is 100% complete before submission. Early applications are highly recommended to avoid portal overload.

Overview

Scholarship to attract meritorious rank-holders of graduation to postgraduate studies in recognized universities.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Scored 1st or 2nd rank in graduation board/university examinations.
  • Age limit below 30 years at the time of PG admission.
  • Enrolled in 1st year Master's degree in recognized university.

Detailed Benefits

Monthly support scholarship directly to help cover university post-graduation expenses.

Documents Required

  • University Rank Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • UG Passing Certificate & mark sheet
  • PG admission details
  • Institution Bonafide Certificate

Application Deadline 15 Dec 2026
