INSPIRE Scholarship for Higher Education (SHE)

⏰ Open

Central • General / OBC / SC / ST

Total Benefit Amount ₹80,000 per year

Important Note: Ensure all documents are scanned clearly and your profile is 100% complete before submission. Early applications are highly recommended to avoid portal overload.

Overview

DST central initiative aiming to attract talented youth to study natural and basic sciences at college level.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Indian National.
  • Top 1% rankers in Class 12 board examinations (State or Central boards).
  • Enrolled in regular B.Sc., B.S., or Int. M.Sc./M.S. in natural/basic sciences.
  • Age limit between 17 and 22 years.

Detailed Benefits

Covers annual academic scholarship of ₹60,000 + ₹20,000 mentorship grant for research projects.

Documents Required

  • Class 12th Marksheet & Passing Certificate
  • Class 10th Certificate
  • Advisory Note / Board Rank Certificate
  • College Admission Proof
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Endorsement Certificate by Principal
