🌟 State vs Central Sector Funding Systems

Students often wonder about the structural differences between state and central initiatives:

  • State Scholarships: Funded entirely by regional state welfare departments. Candidates must possess domicile proof of that specific state.
  • Central Scholarships: Funded by central ministries (Ministry of Minority Affairs, tribal affairs, etc.) and managed through NSP.
  • Double Benefit Restriction: Under strict financial rules, a student cannot draw benefits from two different government scholarships simultaneously! Doing so can lead to benefit recovery and portal suspension.
